Happy Birthday, Baby!

happy birthday. my favorite part of every single day is getting to talk to you. no matter how tired, stressed, or drained i am, hearing your voice, texting you, or even just knowing you’re there makes everything feel okay. i don’t think you realize just how much you mean to me. you’re my safe place, my happiness, my everything.

i just wanna be with you already. wanna hold you, wanna kiss you every minute of every day, wanna make up for all the time we’ve spent apart. i wanna give you the kind of love that makes you feel so safe, so wanted, so completely mine that you never have to question a thing.

i love you, baby. happy birthday. i hope today is everything you deserve and more, because you deserve the absolute best; always.
